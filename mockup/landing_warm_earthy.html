Our Story

A legacy built on
quiet excellence

Founded in 2000 and primarily owned by the Bank Indonesia Employee Welfare Foundation (YKKBI), we began with the flagship Hotel Bidakara Jakarta — and never stopped refining the art of Indonesian hospitality.

Today, Bumikarsa Bidakara Hotels Management is a full-service hospitality partner: property management, HR solutions, reservation systems, and a growing network of associate properties from highland retreats to urban galleries.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted & excellent professional in Indonesian hospitality management.

Our Mission

Delivering consultant services through sustainable, optimal performance — always people-first.
